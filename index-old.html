About Me

Computer Vision and Deep Learning Engineer with 4+ years at Sony Corporation, Tokyo, building real-time AI that ships in AR/VR headsets, manufacturing lines, and retail venues.

I design and deploy end-to-end vision pipelines spanning human-object interaction detection, eye-tracking, action recognition, super-resolution, and FPGA-accelerated rendering for next-generation XR products.

My work sits at the intersection of deep learning research and production engineering, turning state-of-the-art models into low-latency systems that run on mobile SoCs and edge GPUs. I hold an M.Tech from IIT Bombay (1st rank, 9.9/10 CPI) and am passionate about pushing the boundaries of what machines can see and understand in real time.
